Acknowledgments
================

Siena Galaxy Atlas
-------------------

John Moustakas gratefully acknowledges funding support for this work from
the U.S. Department of Energy, Office of Science, Office of High Energy
Physics under Award Number DE-SC0020086.

Use of the SGA data products must cite the following papers:

    Moustakas, J., Lang, D., et al. 2026, in preparation, "Siena Galaxy Atlas 2025"

    Moustakas, J., Lang, D., et al. 2023, ApJS, 269, 3,
    `"Siena Galaxy Atlas 2020" <https://doi.org/10.3847/1538-4365/acfaa2>`_


In addition, users must include the `Scientific Publication
Acknowledgment
<https://www.legacysurvey.org/acknowledgment/#scientific-publication-acknowledgment>`_
for the DESI Legacy Imaging Surveys.

External Data and Tools
-------------------------

We gratefully acknowledge the following external data and tools, which made
the Siena Galaxy Atlas possible.

**NED**

This research has made use of the
`NASA/IPAC Extragalactic Database (NED) <https://ned.ipac.caltech.edu/>`_,
which is funded by the National Aeronautics and Space Administration and
operated by the California Institute of Technology.

**HyperLeda**

We acknowledge use of the
`HyperLeda database <http://leda.univ-lyon1.fr>`_ (`Makarov et al. 2014, A&A, 570, A13 <https://www.aanda.org/articles/aa/full_html/2014/10/aa23496-14/aa23496-14.html>`_).

**DESI**

This research used data obtained with the `Dark Energy Spectroscopic
Instrument (DESI) <https://desi.lbl.gov/>`_:

    `DESI Collaboration, et al. 2026, AJ, 171, 285, "Data Release 1 of
    the Dark Energy Spectroscopic Instrument"
    <https://arxiv.org/abs/2503.14745>`_

See the
`DESI acknowledgments page <https://data.desi.lbl.gov/public/doc/acknowledgments/#acknowledgments>`_
for full details.

**SDSS**

This research made use of data from the `Sloan Digital Sky Survey Data
Release 17 <https://www.sdss4.org/dr17/>`_ (`Abdurro'uf et al. 2022,
ApJS, 259, 35
<https://iopscience.iop.org/article/10.3847/1538-4365/ac4414>`_). See
`sdss4.org <https://www.sdss4.org>`_ for additional details.

**Local Volume Database (LVD)**

We made use of the
`Local Volume Database <https://local-volume-database.readthedocs.io/en/latest/index.html>`_:

    `Pace, A. B. 2025, The Open Journal of Astrophysics, 8, 144859,
    "The Local Volume Database: A Library of the Observed Properties
    of Nearby Dwarf Galaxies and Star Clusters"
    <https://doi.org/10.33232/001c.144859>`_

Feedback
--------

We welcome questions and feedback from users, as well as requests for
additional data products, through the ticket system at
`github.com/moustakas/SGA/issues <https://github.com/moustakas/SGA/issues>`_.

All of the code used to select, build, and analyze the catalog is open
source and publicly available:

- `github.com/moustakas/SGA <https://github.com/moustakas/SGA>`_
- `github.com/legacysurvey/legacypipe <https://github.com/legacysurvey/legacypipe>`_
